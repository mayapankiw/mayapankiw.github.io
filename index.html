Overview

I am a Mechanical and Biomedical Engineering graduate from McMaster University and a current graduate student at the University of Toronto, working in the Cardiac Catheterization Research Laboratory at Mount Sinai Hospital. My work integrates cardiac engineering, hemodynamic modelling, invasive waveform analysis, and signal processing to develop clinically relevant tools for cardiovascular assessment.

This portfolio highlights projects in physiologic signal processing, cardiac research, biomechanical design, and scholarly work.
