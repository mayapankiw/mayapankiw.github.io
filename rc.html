Remote-Controlled Vehicle Design

CAD/CAM/CAE Project

Designed a fully functional remote-controlled vehicle using Autodesk Inventor, integrating mechanical design, simulation, and manufacturing workflows.

  • Designed full vehicle assembly including drivetrain and gear system
  • Created engineering drawings and bill of materials (BOM)
  • Performed stress simulations and mesh convergence analysis
  • Conducted motion studies to validate mechanical performance
  • Applied topology optimization to reduce mass while maintaining strength
