About Me

I am a graduate student at the University of Toronto working in the Cardiac Catheterization Research Laboratory at Mount Sinai Hospital. My work focuses on cardiac engineering, hemodynamic modelling, invasive waveform analysis, and signal processing for clinically relevant applications.

My current research centers on the development of an algorithm to estimate right ventricular–pulmonary arterial coupling from invasive pressure data collected during a right heart catheterization, with the goal of improving physiologic assessment in advanced heart failure care.
